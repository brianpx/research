QI PROJECT

Improving Access to Anesthesiology Research Opportunities

A Centralized, Role-Based Online Signup System for Learners

Background / Problem Statement

Currently, residents and medical students in the Department of Anesthesiology and Pain Medicine rely on ad hoc networking or word-of-mouth to learn about available research and QI projects. This lack of centralized, up-to-date access creates inefficiencies, delays project recruitment, and may reduce learner engagement in scholarly activity.

A centralized, easily accessible, and role-based signup system linked from the Department's Research Listing could improve visibility, streamline communication between PIs and potential learners, and increase participation in scholarly projects.

QI Aim Statement

To increase the proportion of residents and medical students successfully matched to active anesthesiology research/QI projects by 50% within 6 months of implementing a centralized, role-based online signup system.
